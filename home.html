SDITS,the first Technical Institute in East Nimar Region,was started in 2004. Institute functions under the patronage of Dadaji
Dhuniwale Professional Education And Development Co-operative Society Limited Khandwa & is managed by Technocrats who
have excelled intheir respective profession. Since inception it is endeavoring for the quality and excellence in the technical education.

SDITS has earned reputation not only in the”NIMAR” region but also in the entire state of Madhya Pradesh. Founder Chairman of society, Late Shri Tanwant Singh Keer (Ex. Minister), has put his effort to promote quality education along with Shri Rana Sajjan Singh an Agriculturist.

S.D.I.T.S is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Technical University as pronounced RGPV, is the State Govt. authorized Technical University. This University is established in the year 1998, by Madhya Pradesh VidhanSabha Act 13, 1998.
